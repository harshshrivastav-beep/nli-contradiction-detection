Abstract

This system leverages BART-Large fine-tuned on the MNLI corpus to perform Natural Language Inference (NLI) ‚Äî a core task in NLP that determines the semantic relationship between a premise and a hypothesis. Given any two sentences, the model classifies their relationship as Entailment, Contradiction, or Neutral, along with a confidence score derived from the softmax probability distribution.
